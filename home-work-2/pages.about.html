About us

We have been serving delicious coffee for two years in a stylish and comfortable setting. Additionally, our coffee shop is conveniently located for easy access.

Our coffee house offers a signature blend of 80% Arabica and 20% Robusta, crafted for true coffee connoisseurs. However,if you prefer pure Arabica or Robusta, we are happy to prepare it for you.
